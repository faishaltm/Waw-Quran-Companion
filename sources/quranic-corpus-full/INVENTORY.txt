FILES

1. quranic-corpus-dependencies-full.txt (1.7MB, 37,420 lines)
   - Dependency treebank with parent-child relationships
   - 47 relation types with Arabic labels
   - Format: CHILD | PARENT | RELATION | ARABIC | ENGLISH
   - Example: (1:1:3) → (1:1:2) | adj | صفة | Adjective

2. quranic-corpus-morphology-full.txt (2.6MB, 77,429 lines)
   - Complete morphological analysis of every word
   - POS tags, lemmas, roots, gender, number, case
   - Format: Features space-delimited on single line
   - Example: bi+ POS:N LEM:{som ROOT:smw M GEN

3. quranic-corpus-named-entities-full.txt (130KB, 5,494 lines)
   - Named entity and concept annotations
   - Links words to semantic concepts
   - Format: LOCATION | TYPE:CONCEPT_ID
   - Example: (1:1:2:1) | CON:allah

4. quranic-corpus-lemmas.txt (40KB, 1,593 lines)
   - Complete vocabulary of the Quran
   - One lemma per line in Buckwalter
   - Example: {som, {ll~ah, r~aHoma`n

5. quranic-corpus-pause-marks.txt (38KB, 4,359 lines)
   - Tajweed recitation pause marks
   - Format: LOCATION | PAUSE_TYPE
   - Example: (1:1:4) | m

6. README.md (20KB)
